About "Days Before Rodeo"

Days Before Rodeo is the second mixtape by American rapper Travis Scott. It was released for free on August 18, 2014 by Grand Hustle Records. Serving as a prequel to Rodeo. Travis Scott released a second, third and fourth deluxe version of the mixtape to his website on August 29, 2024, featuring an additional two tracks. Ten years after it’s release, the mixtape saw a re-release on all platforms.
